About

Hi, I'm Miguel Cabrera.

I am a front-end developer with a passion for building and designing. I recently graduated from Juno Colleges immersive Bootcamp for Web Development. From this experience, I gained a strong understanding and proficiency in HTML5, CSS, JavaScript, React, Firebase, SCSS, and Git to name a few.

Before this Bootcamp, I graduated in Architectural Technology with Honours in 2015 & shortly after, I worked for a tech company that specialized in 3D-modeling and VR (Virtual Reality). Being in this field taught me how to respond effectively when working in a high-pressure environment. It also guided me to be a valuable team player and communicate well with others.

With these experiences, I have grown dedicated to my craft and aim to create websites that provide clients with intuitive designs, functionality and accessibility. Coding has allowed me to express my creativity and develop a strong foundation to build on. I am excited to begin this new journey with a positive attitude and an open mindset.
